German declension practice
Declension in German is separated into three kinds: strong, mixed, and weak.
Strong declesion is used with der-like determiners, such as der/die/das, welch(er), jed(er), jen(er), etc.
Mixed declension is used with ein-like determiners, such as ein/eine, kein(e), irgend(ein) and possessives.
Weak declension is used with adjectives.
